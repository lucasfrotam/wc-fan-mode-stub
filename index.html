World Cup Fan Mode

DACH-market insight: Tipmaster fans tip 8 matches per Cup Run. Endurance athletes lose sleep on 22:00 CET kickoffs. This stub reshuffles your week and exports a Portable AI Coach prompt — same pattern as AiTrainingPlan's core workflow.

Product judgement: Tipmaster owns predictions; AiTrainingPlan owns the body. Overlap audience = German football fans who also run marathons. Neither product addresses tournament-month training disruption — this feature stub fills that gap without copying either UI.
